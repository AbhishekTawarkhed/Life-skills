# Listening and Active Communication

## 1. Active Listening
- Paying attention
- Show I am listening
- Reflect and paraphrase
- Ask clarifying questions
- Avoid interrupting
- Respond appropriately

## 2. Reflective Listening
Reflective listening means repeating back what one heard, checking if they got it right, and showing that one understand both the words and the emotions behind them.  
Key points (Fisher’s model):
- Restating the message
- Mirroring emotions
- Clarifying meaning
- Validating the speaker
- Summarizing

## 3. Reflection
**Obstacles in my listening process:**
- Thinking about my response
- Noise/multitasking
- Biases
- Losing focus
- Being judgemental

## 4. Improvement in Listening
**Ways to improve:**
- Take notes
- Paraphrase
- Reduce distractions
- Be patient
- Listen for emotions
