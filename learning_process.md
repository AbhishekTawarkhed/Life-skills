## What is the Feynman Technique?
Explaining a concept in simple language as if teaching it to someone else.

## Active vs. Diffused Modes of Thinking: 
Active Mode: Focused, intense concentration on a problem or concept.  
Diffused Mode:Relaxed, broad thinking where the brain makes connections subconsciously, often leading to creative insights. 

## Steps to take in approaching a new topic 
1. Deconstruct the skill into smaller parts.  
2. Learn enough to self-correct.  
3. Remove practice barriers (distractions).  
4. Practice at least 20 hours.  


## Actions to improve your learning process going forward :  
practice deep work: schedule distraction-free focus sessions. 
- Apply the Feynman Technique: explain concepts in your own words.  
- Alternate between active and diffused modes: focus deeply, then relax to allow creative connections.  
- Break down new topics into smaller chunks and commit to 20 hours of focused practice.
