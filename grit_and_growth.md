### 1 Grit
Angela Duckworth explains that grit—passion and perseverance over the long term—is a stronger predictor of success than talent or intelligence. It’s about sticking with goals despite setbacks and staying committed until the end.  

---

### 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset
I learnt that a growth mindset means believing abilities can be developed through effort, practice, and learning. Challenges and failures are opportunities to grow rather than signs of limitation.  

---

### 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control
-  Internal locus of control is the belief that your actions and decisions directly shape your outcomes, rather than luck or external forces.  
-  Motivation and success come from realizing that you are in charge of your life—owning responsibility empowers you to stay consistent and achieve goals.  

---

### 4. How to build a Growth Mindset 
- Believe you can figure things out  
- Question your assumptions  
- Create your own learning path  
- Honor the struggle  

---

### 5. Mindset - A MountBlue Warrior Reference Manual
